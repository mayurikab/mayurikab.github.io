Reddit Accessibility Overlay

Browser extension improving readability and cognitive accessibility for long-form discussion threads. Includes AI thread summarization, text-to-speech, and a reduced stimulation mode for users with ADHD and dyslexia. Evaluated through an 8-participant within-subjects user study, with the largest gains in comprehension and task time for participants with ADHD and/or dyslexia.

Neurodiversity in Software Engineering (Research)

Literature review on cognitive differences and developer experience, synthesizing findings across 10+ empirical and systematic studies to identify gaps at the intersection of cognitive science and software engineering workflows.

Human Activity Recognition

Synthetic IMU data generation pipeline for wrist-worn activity sensing, built on discretized Bio-PM movement embeddings and an autoregressive GPT trained to generate realistic motion sequences. Used to augment training data for activity classification under strict subject-disjoint evaluation.
