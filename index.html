Mayurika Borah

Software Engineer · M.S. Computer Science

I am a Master's student in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, thinking about human-centered design and development, accessibility, and machine learning. Before this, I worked for 2 years as a Software Development Engineer at Expedia, building data pipelines and cloud infrastructure for their platform team. Lately, I've been drawn to questions about how technology fits (or doesn't) around the way different minds actually work. I've built an accessibility extension for Reddit, played around with synthetic data generation for wearable health sensors, and spent a while reading everything I could find on neurodivergent developers' experiences in software engineering. Outside of that, I like dabbling in foreign languages and studying human physiology.

Research Interests

Human-Computer Interaction, Accessibility, Neurodiversity in Computing, Machine Learning, Digital Health
